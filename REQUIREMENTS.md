# System Requirements

To successfully run and develop **How I Met Your Tracker**, ensure your system meets the following requirements:

## Minimum Requirements (For Users)
- **Browser**: Google Chrome Version 88 or higher (Manifest V3 support required).
- **OS**: Windows, macOS, or Linux.
- **Visuals**: A screen resolution of at least 1280x720 is recommended for the dashboard.

## Development Requirements (For Contributors)
- **Node.js**: Version 18.0.0 or higher (LTS recommended).
- **npm**: Version 9.0.0 or higher.
- **Git**: For version control.
- **Code Editor**: VS Code (recommended) or any standard IDE.
